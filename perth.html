EV Charger Installation in Perth, WA

Everything Perth EV owners need to know about home charger installation.

Perth's Growing EV Market

Perth is one of Australia's fastest-growing EV markets. With Western Australia's sunny climate and the popularity of Tesla, BYD, and Hyundai Ioniq models, demand for home EV charger installation has skyrocketed.

A dedicated home charger lets you wake up to a full battery every morning — no public charging queues, no range anxiety. Most Perth EV owners charge overnight on an off-peak electricity tariff.

WA Electrical Licensing Requirements

In Western Australia, all electrical work must be carried out by an Electrical Contractor licensed by the Electrical Licensing Board of WA (ELB). Our electricians hold current WA electrical contractor licences and carry full public liability insurance.

After installation, you receive an Electrical Safety Certificate — required for insurance and for any future property transactions.

Perth Pricing

Perth EV charger installation costs typically range from $800–$1,200 for a standard single-phase installation. Three-phase installations run $1,200–$2,000. Older homes in suburbs like Subiaco, Fremantle, and Cottesloe may require switchboard upgrades, which add $800–$2,000 to the job.

We provide fixed-price quotes before any work begins — no surprises on invoice day.

WA Government Rebates

Western Australia currently offers EV incentives including stamp duty exemptions on new EVs and planned charging infrastructure rebates. Western Power also has programs supporting residential EV adoption. Our team stays up to date on available incentives and will advise you during your quote.
